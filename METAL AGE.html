The Metal Age

The Metal Age is the period where man learned and started
to use iron implements, tools, and weapons. The extensive
use of iron coincided with the changes
in agricultural practices and other changes in society,
such as religious beliefs and arts.

Modern archaeological findings recognizes the creation of
iron implements tp have taken place in Anatolia in 1200 BCE.
This development of iron materials was credited to the
Hittites of Anatolia.
